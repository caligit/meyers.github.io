About Meyers Construction & Consulting

Our roofing company for the greater St. Louis area specializes in re-roofing, roof repair, maintenance programs/packages, storm damaged roofs & new roof installs. We offer free quotes and are available 24/7.

Meyers Construction and Consulting has 10 years of experience in the industry, and are professionals at assessing storm damage, such as hail or wind and helping you put a new roof over your head while working with your insurance company to do so. We are fully licensed and insured so you can rest assured your roofing work is being carried out professionally.

We understand how important your home or business is. We provide honest, quality and professional roofing services to get you back in shape and to give you the peace of mind you need. Our work is carried out as if it were our own home or business, by our professional and experienced roofers. You can be sure all of our roofing products used are industry-standard with extensive warranties to keep you covered for many years to come.

Our specialty is flat roofing; however, regardless of your roofing type, you can rest assured that we’ll get the job done right – on time, and on budget. Our professional flat roofing contractors are committed to providing you only the highest quality service at a price you can afford. Highly experienced in both residential and commercial, roofing estimates are always free and are guaranteed in writing.
